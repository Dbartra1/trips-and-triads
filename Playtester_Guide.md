# Trips & Triads - Playtester Quick Start Guide

Welcome to the playtest! This guide will quickly get you up to speed on the core mechanics, economy, and flow of the game. 

---

## 🎮 Basic Game Flow
1. **Pre-Match Screen**: Select a district, build your 5-card deck, and choose your mission.
2. **The Match**: A 3x3 grid tactical card game. Play cards to capture opponent cards by matching higher stats on adjacent sides. 
3. **Post-Match**: Resolve stakes (win/loss rewards), gain Street Cred, earn Scrip, and return to the Pre-Match screen.

## 🃏 Deck Building
- You must select **exactly 5 cards** to start a match.
- Your deck must contain **1 Hero** and up to **4 Support cards** (Pro or Street tier).
- *Note: If you try to start a match with fewer than 5 cards, the deck counter will flash a warning.*

## ⚔️ Hero Capture & Reclaim (The Hunt)
- If your Hero is captured, you become **Headless** and a **Hunt** begins.
- You have **2 Reclaim Attempts** to duel the captor and win your Hero back.
- **Step Up**: If you fail or choose to, you can promote a Support card from your roster to temporarily act as your Hero for the Reclaim duel.
- **Buyout**: You can spend **Scrip** to ransom your Hero back without fighting (cost varies by faction and attempts remaining).

## 🎯 Match Stakes
Every district has a "Stake" that determines what you win or lose:
- **OneJob**: Win = take 1 random enemy card. Lose = lose 1 random card from your board.
- **The Spread**: Win/Lose cards equal to the point margin of the match.
- **As Flipped**: You keep any enemy cards you successfully captured during the match.
- **Everything**: Winner takes ALL cards the loser brought to the match (board + hand). *High risk, high reward.*

## 🌟 Hero Modifiers
Heroes have unique passive abilities and "Domains" that alter the rules:
- **Domains**: Grant stat bonuses to specific adjacent cards (e.g., Aegis, Killzone).
- **Abilities**: Unique rules like *Decay* (Vesna), *Listener* (Riven refuses to capture Choir cards), or *The Rivalry* (special head-to-head resolution between specific heroes).

## 💰 Scrip & The Economy
**Scrip** is the campaign currency. You need it to grow your crew and survive.
- **Earning**: Win matches (especially District matches), complete Della Contracts.
- **Spending**: Recruit new Free Agents, Buyout captured Heroes, and (in future updates) pay crew Upkeep.

## 🛠️ Della Contracts (Standing Work)
- Found in the **Della** tab on the Pre-Match screen.
- These are low-risk, base-rule matches that pay a flat Scrip reward (scaled by your Street Cred).
- **Limit**: You only get **3 Contracts** per district cycle. 
- **Refresh**: Playing a standard District Match will reset your Della Contracts back to 3.

## 📋 Recruitment System
Want to expand your roster? Use the **Recruitment** tab to scout Free Agents.
1. **Meet (5 Scrip)**: Reveals the agent's name and stats.
2. **Audition (10 Scrip)**: Tests the agent. *Note: There is a small (10%) chance they will fail and refuse to join.*
3. **Sign (15 Scrip)**: If they pass the audition, pay the final fee to add them permanently to your roster.
- *The recruitment board refreshes with new agents after every standard District Match.*

## 🗺️ District Modifiers Quick Reference
Each district applies unique rules to the match. Here is what to expect:

| Modifier | What it Does |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Intercept** | You can see the AI's hand before the match begins. |
| **Conscription** | You do *not* pick your deck. The game randomly selects 5 cards from your roster for you. |
| **Standoff** | If the match ends in a tie, it immediately restarts using the current board state (no deck redraw). |
| **Overflow** | Capturing a card via a Protocol triggers a chain reaction, allowing you to capture adjacent enemy cards with your newly captured card. |
| **Protocols** | Special capture rules: <br>• **Handshake**: Captures if adjacent stats are nearly equal.<br>• **Tally**: Captures if the sum of adjacent stats matches.<br>• **Wall Signature**: Captures if a specific high-stat "wall" is present. |

### District Rule Breakdown
- **The Stub**: Base rules only. (Tutorial district)
- **Glass Spire**: Intercept + Protocols (Wall Signature, Handshake)
- **The Killfloor**: Conscription + Standoff
- **Dead Channel**: Intercept + Overflow
- **The Sprawl-Market**: Conscription
- **The Powder Room**: Protocols (Tally, Handshake)
- **The Hush**: Overflow + Protocols (Wall Signature, Handshake)
- **The Vault**: Intercept + Conscription + Standoff + Overflow + All Protocols *(The ultimate challenge)*
